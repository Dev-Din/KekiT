About KeKiT

Handmade cupcake gifts delivered to your door. A yummy alternative to flowers, we offer our luxury cupcake delivery nationwide across the Kenya. Not only do we offer a gift that is a little different â€“ the way we do it is pretty special too. Our cupcakes are sold in gift boxes of 6 and 12, each box includes a gift message and is tied with a ribbon. Orders placed by 4 pm can be delivered the next day (Tuesday to Saturday). All orders are delivered by fragile courier service; fully trackable and the fastest way to get your gift to its destination. If you are looking for a convenient and reliable way to get cupcakes delivered, for yourself, or as a thoughtful gift, we can help.

We also cater for dietary requirements such as gluten-free cupcakes and vegan cupcakes.

Not sure what flavour to pick for your cupcake delivery? Check out our selection boxes of complementary flavours or browse by Occasion. Our Tasting Menu Cupcakes includes a selection of our most popular flavours. Want something more personal? You can have your cupcakes personalised with our Message Cupcakes service or our Photo Cupcakes service. Choosing us to enhance a celebration is a privilege, we exist to send cupcakes that are as delicious as they are beautifully presented. Our cupcakes delivered to you or as a present are a complete gift, designed to delight.

You can even add some Little Extras to make your gift extra special!

To find out more information on our extensive menu, or for anything about our cupcake delivery options, do not hesitate to contact us. We are always happy to help.
